
### **Assignment:**

1. **Log Rotation Script**:  
   Write a script that rotates logs in a specific directory, compressing logs older than 30 days and deleting logs older than 90 days. 🔄

2. **System Monitoring Script**:  
   Write a script that checks the CPU usage and disk usage. If either exceeds 80%, send an email alert to the administrator. ⚠️

3. **Backup Strategy Script**:  
   Create a backup script that performs:
   - Full backups every Sunday 🌞
   - Incremental backups for the rest of the week 📆
   - Sends email alerts for successful or failed backups 📧
